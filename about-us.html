Nuri Edwardo

Vision & Story

Hello and welcome. My name is Nuri Edwardo and I’m the founder of NTNK. After traveling the world, exploring over 50 countries, and achieving financial freedom for myself, I decided to establish an online community where like-minded people can thrive and grow together.

Preview About Me Video

NTNK was founded in 2019.

Aged 4 he made his first sale, and at 12 he launched his first e-commerce business. He made his first online profits at the age of 14 and started to develop various products over the past 2 decades. Through his entrepreneurial journey, Nuri says that WHAT HE VALUES MOST IS WHAT HE LEARNED ALONG THE WAY.

After traveling the world, exploring over 50 countries, and achieving financial freedom for himself, Nuri realized the critical importance of teamwork, so he decided to establish an online community where like-minded people can THRIVE AND GROW TOGETHER.

He was a solopreneur for decades, operating most functions of the business himself. His discipline and determination provided a progressive path to success, but the road was long and challenging. Finding optimal solutions and qualified collaboration partners was a constant struggle. This experience gave him the motivation to help others to find a better way. With that spirit, the NTNK journey began.

The Brand.

NTNK doesn't only offer services and a valuable network-based platform, we also have our own lifestyle brand, DO YOU by NTNK. Our products embody the values, interests, and attitudes of our company.

We believe in combining function with aesthetics and creating products of high quality designed to last and survive passing trends. We also believe that the best design is defined by the details. NTNK design is timeless and sophisticated yet has a lot of character. Our goal is to offer products that fit the NTNK lifestyle and cause minimal impact on the planet.

The Lifestyle Collection.

Our products are designed by our network and are used by our community, as well as thousands happy customers around the world. We wear and use our products ourselves because we trust and appreciate the quality, functionality, and detail that our international team-members put into their design and development processes.

Our product range consists of a lifestyle collection, and we’ll grow it together, by integrating our customers' feedback into our product development. Being responsive to customer feedback is a priority. We don't just want to make products and sell products but to create genuine value and that's what defines our company.
